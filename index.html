Our Story

Our company was established in 1995. The genuine commitment of all our staff to our business and their dedication to high standard service has provided the main goal of the Company – the wide range of offerings. Through own branches and agency network the Company ensures the “from door to door” service by any means of transport.

Our carrier’s responsibility is guaranteed by the cooperation with the leading insurance companies and membership of authoritative international organizations and associations.

We Offer

  • Transportation of goods from any point of the world to Georgia and South Caucasus countries by the sea, land, railway and air transport.

  • Optimal logistical planning of movement of OGC, heavy items, dangerous and specific cargo.

  • Handling of bulk cargo in Poti and Batumi seaports.

  • Consolidation and transportation of LCL from Europe, America and China.

  • Transportation of cargo with temperature regime and warehousing.

  • Vessel charter and shipping

  • Cargo insurance.

Our Services

Railway Transport

GA LOGISTICS has got the name of a reliable railway carrier from Poti and Batumi seaports to the Caucasus and Central Asian countries. GA LOGISTICS offers the cargo export and import affordable railway, forwarding and transportation services from/to any point of Europe, CIS, Central Asia and China.

We offer you the organizing multimodal transportation via Poti and Batumi seaports. We accept, transship and reliably fasten the cargo on railway wagon in order to transport it to the place of destination.

Air Transport

Air transportation is the most rapid, safe but expensive service as well. GA LOGISTICS cooperates with several leading airlines that make our tariffs quite competitive. We offer consolidated and charter air transportation (both import and export) via Tbilisi International Airport (TBS).

Containers

GA LOGISTICS offers container transport to all destinations. As a result of our long-term cooperation with leading shipping companies we have developed the best service and competitive prices for sea transportation. We perform inland transportation by our own vehicles that makes our service flexible and prices – competitive. GA LOGISTICS high-skilled staff offers: – container transportation – sea/inland, import, export, transit; – transport of special cargo by special containers; – transport of perishable goods by refrigerator containers and warehousing of cargo if necessary; – cargo insurance; – Brokerage. We offer the instant tracking by the container, bill of lading or vehicle number on the web-site or through the operator. For more information please contact info@gal.ge

Project Cargo

Project cargo (OOG, oversize, heavy items, bulk) is the most successful activity for GA Logistics. Our officers have great experience in handling of the following cargo:

  • Iron sheets, rolled steel sections, iron wiring, metal pipes and various metal constructions.
  • Turbines, transformers, large-size equipment, aircraft stairways, oil extraction units, mobile ICUs and fire-engines.
  • Timber products: logs, rolled paper, wood.
  • Track machines: bulldozers, graders, tractors.
  • Construction equipment: drilling machines, lifting mechanisms, excavators and so on
  • Dry bulk cargo.
Our experienced and trained team offers:
  • Determination of oversize grade.
  • Design and engineering calculations.
  • Planning of transportation mode.
  • Loading, fastening, separation service.
  • Organization of issue of licenses and permits required for transportation within Georgia and all countries involved in transportation
  • Escorting
  • Insurance
For additional information and other specific details please contact info@gal.ge

Inland Transport

GA Logistics offers inland transportation by means of own trucks that are equipped whit GPRS system. We offer tents of all volume as well as trailers with temperature regime. In case of your wish we can offer the transportation with double temperature regime (one trailer will keep both “+” and “_” regime). We operate in Europe, South Caucasus, Russia and Central Asia. Transportation of consolidated (LCL) cargo implies cargo forwarding from any country under the “from door to door” condition. Cargo is consolidated in Italy, the city of Bologna, Ravenna or Trieste warehouses. Under mentioned condition we transport both ordinary and dangerous (of ADR class) cargo. As one of the leading transport and logistic companies we ensure transportation of cargo by the most effective route to the point of destination, by safe and time manner. Contact us! Our inland transport experts will be glad to help you. Info@gal.ge
